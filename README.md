## Project Overview
### Introduction
An interdisciplinary case competition jointly organised by NUS DSESC and NTU ECDS Club.

Databusters leverages the cross-disciplinary training of DSE and ECDS students as an analytical foundation, while enabling teams to incorporate diverse perspectives from other fields. By working with real-world datasets and applied problem statements, participants integrate data-driven analysis with economic reasoning to produce rigorous, actionable insights relevant to industry and public-sector decision-making.

### Covered topics
* 2008 financial crash
* 2022 Terra Luna crash
